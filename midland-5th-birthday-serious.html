Our House 5th Birthday

I asked to design Exeter promoter, Our House's 5th Birthday event which was an extensive project that required me to produce a range assets for both print and digital. My designs were reproduced across A0/A1 posters, A3 flyers, physical tickets for the events, t-shirts, GIFs and social media banners. I was even lucky enough to have my designs featured in Exeter's Etcetera magazine.
