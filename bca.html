BCA School of English

Brand Identity and Website

Breifed by BCA School of English in to create a logo, brand identity and website.

Their existing logo hadn’t been updated for some years and they had no overarching brand identity. The brief was for a logo that felt fresh, young and spoke to the school’s international nature.

The identity concept is based on using the geometric shapes and grids used in flags. These shapes are used in the logo and in the creation of grids that can be used on for other assets including easily updatable poster templates and the website.
