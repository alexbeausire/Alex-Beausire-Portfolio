Vauxhaull Arches

Brand Identity and Logo Design

Logo and branding for Vauxhaull Arches, a corporate events space that is part of the Fire and Lightbox site.

The Vauxhaull Arches brand was launched to promote the site as a corporate events space for product launches, conventions and other similiar events.

The identity takes inspiration from the four venues that make up the site, each in its own archway.
